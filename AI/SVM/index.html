SVM Lab

Press 'p' for adding positive labeled under your mouse position.

Press 'q' for adding negative labeled under your mouse position.

Press 'Enter' for starting a learning process or to pause an ongoing one.

Press 'Reset' for erasing all data and learning data

Press 'Set Best SVM' to carry on learning from the Best SVM

Press 'Reset Learning' to erase all learning data but NOT datapoints
